Accident Prevention System

  • In Rainy Weather: Reduce speed, increase braking distance, and use headlights.
  • In Foggy Conditions: Use fog lights, avoid high beams, and drive slowly.
  • On Highways: Maintain lane discipline, avoid sudden braking, and monitor blind spots.
  • In High Traffic: Be patient, avoid lane switching, and keep a safe distance.
  • For High Vehicle Speeds: Observe speed limits, especially on curves and wet surfaces.
  • In Snowy Weather: Use snow tires, drive slowly, and avoid sudden turns or stops.
